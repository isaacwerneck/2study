welcome to my void in space!

hi there! looks like you stumbled into my digital dimension! no worries, you're safe here :) this isn't a particularly interesting place — this is simply a space inhabited by a queer canine looking for some peace and quiet on the web. feel free to take a look around if you wanna get to know me or see my creations! be warned that i love to ramble, so you'll see plenty of pages that seem... wordier than they need to be. i try to use abbreviations when necessary to help make everything look at least a little neater!

i try to make my website fun but also accessible! i only manage this site as a hobby, so if there is anything i can do to improve accessibility, or if there are any bugs or other issues, the best way to contact me would be to email me *

here's a portal to my home on the gemini protocol!

this website is under construction, so some pages may appear incomplete or blank!

website notes!

  • this was coded in the native code editor of neocities nekoweb on a firefox-based browser in 120% zoom using a 1920x1080 display size!
  • made with both computer and mobile in mind! preferable to view on desktop for the full experience, but still very mobile functional with a few imperfections!
  • this website contains and requires the enabling of both iframes and javascript!
  • load times may be slow at times since sometimes there's plenty of stuff going on... sorry for the inconvenience!
